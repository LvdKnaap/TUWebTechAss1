Instructions:

  1. Take turns rolling the dice,
  2. Save any dice you want and re-roll the other dice,
  3. You have a maximum of three rolls of the dice,
  4. Choose one of the 13 scoring categories,
  5. The score per category depends on the outcome of the dice,
  6. For details regarding the scoring rules, see the top link,
  7. The game ends once both players have selected all categories,
  8. The player with the most points is declared the winner.
